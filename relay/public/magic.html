Connection Troubleshooting
  • Ensure your phone is connected to your **home Wi-Fi network** (not cellular data).
  • Verify the TV's IP address matches exactly under **Settings > Network > About** on your Roku screen.
  • Some routers block local network communication (AP/Client Isolation). If so, manual IP pairing might be needed.
How it works:
Your phone sends the play command over your home Wi-Fi network directly to the TV. No account sign-in or installation required.
