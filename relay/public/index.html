Welcome to Quickbeam! ⚡️

Quickbeam is a simple, lightweight utility that lets you instantly send and play videos on a friend's Roku TV—completely free, with no accounts or sign-ins needed.

1

Get the Roku App: Install and open the Quickbeam channel on the Roku TV. It will display a 6-digit pairing code on the screen.

2

Add Contact & Pair: Under "Quick Send", add your contact's name and their 6-digit TV pairing code. You can also save their phone or email for direct delivery.

3

Cast Instantly: Paste a video URL, tap their contact card, and send it! Opening the link instantly launches the video on their TV.
